Who We Are

Building the Future of Technology

Founded in 2018, MLK Tech has grown from a small startup into a leading technology company and training institute. We bridge the gap between industry needs and talent by delivering innovative software solutions and comprehensive IT education.

Our team of 50+ engineers, designers, and educators work collaboratively to help businesses digitize their operations and empower individuals with the skills needed to thrive in the tech industry.
